THRISSUR

in God's own city Kerala

Thrissur is wonderful place locate in the gods on country Kerala. It is know all over the world for its festivals. Thrissur is known as "cultural capital of Kerala" the because of its cultural, spiritual and religious leanings throughout history. One of its main cultural events is the Thrissur Pooram, which attracts quite a number of tourists and travellers. Thrissur Pooram is an annual Hindu festival held in Kerala, India. It is held at the Vadakkunnathan Temple in Thrissur every year on the Pooram day. The people of Thrissur have friendly and welcoming nature.
