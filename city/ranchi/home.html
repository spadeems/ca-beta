About the City

Ranchi

Ranchi is the capital city of Jharkhand. It's one of the most industrial significant in South West of India. Ranchi has a milieu of pan India culture and surrounded with scenic beauty and waterfalls. City is home for many vintage buildings since the British era and Hill station serving the purpose of tourism. Ranchi is also a sports hub which has given India's most successful cricket team caption MS Dhoni, International Archer Deepika Kumari and many international hockey players.
Ranchi is influenced by multiple cultures hence almost all the festivals are celebrated with utmost zeal. Ranchi is educational hub of Jharkhand and hosts institute of National Importance like IIM and NIFT. Prestigious BIT Mesra is also located here. In 2000 after the foundation of Jharkhand Ranchi became the capital of Jharkhand. Ranchi name was derived from Ranch tribal group and hence Ranchi has a unique representation of tribal legacy and urban development aura.

Food of Ranchi

The common Jharkhandi/ Ranchi meal consists of roti, sabji, rice, and achar which is prepared in every household. The food of Jharkhand, however consists of many more delectable items like litti chokha, pittha, handia, rugra, chilka roti, malpua, bamboo shoot, thekua, dyakha, aloo chokha, til barfi and mitha khaja. Talking about a very common food in Ranchi is dhuska, which are deep fried rice flour pancakes that must be served with gram curry and potato.
