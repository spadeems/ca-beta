About the Program

CityAmbassador

The SPADE EMS City Ambassador Program is a platform for students who are passionate about programming to start and run an active programming club in their city. A SPADE EMS City Ambassador is a leader with a keen interest in starting and building a programming community.
You will be the face, voice, and evangelist of SPADE EMS at your city. You will be the champion of our vision for hosting great programming contests, hackathons and building the largest network of developers all around the globe.
