CITY HIGHLIGHTS

ONE OF THE MOST MODERN VIBRANT AND EXCITING CITIES IN INDIA

Jalandhar formally known as 'Jullundhar' is an ancient city of Punjab is a highly industrialized center today. Jalandhar region has a rich tradition of learning and educating right and antiquity. Following its past tradition the city has become a hub education in North India. The city boasts of a unique combination of historic cultural and social facilities.

CITY OF 3C’S

It is the city known for 3c’s i.e Cuisine, Climate and Culture. The city pride itself for having scores of eating joints dishes out continental, Italian, oriental and other varieties of cuisines. A perfect blend of people of different religions and customs, The city has a rich culture and is well known for its hospitality and assimilative power. The city experience a wide climatic variation.

SPORTS

Now jalandhar is a highly industrialized center known as the town of sports. The sports material manufactured in jalandhar and are exported to various countries. The city also has distinction of producing some of the best sportsman in the country. The neighboring city sansarpur is known as the nursery of hockey players. And maximum no. of players are from that village.

WEATHER

The weather here is very brutal, in summer and winter it will test you to the core. In rainy it hardly rains for 10-20 days. Humidity level is very high.The city experience a wide climatic variation. The year may be divided into winter, summer , monsoon and post monsoon seasons of transition period.

SOCIAL LIFE

People lived there are very friendly and cheerful. They will help you in your needs but from my personal experience I think that youngsters are deviated from their paths they only focus on their looks, bikes and enjoyment
